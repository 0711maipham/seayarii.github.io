About

My name's Tonette! I'm a graduate from one of the country's top STEM universities. I'm artistically inclined, and I've been holding a crayon or other drawing utensil ever since I left the womb (my motto is draw first, breath second!).

I'm trained in many sciences including chemistry, animal biology, and ecology. I'm also an emerging .NET web developer with JavaScript, C#, and AngularJS under my belt. My dream is to make life easier for other people. If I can make the world a better place doing what I love, then I couldn't ask for more. Art, science, and coding aren't mutually exclusive and I hope you can agree with me. Feel free to drop a message if you'd like to share tips, stories, or just want to have a chat!
